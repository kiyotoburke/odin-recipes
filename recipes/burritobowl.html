Burrito Bowl

Description

Hello! Welcome to my burrito bowl recipe. On this page I'll show you how to make a delicious and nutricious bowl that is easy to make and easy to customize!

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Rice
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa
  • Grilled Chicken
  • Corn

Steps

  1. First, create a base layer with your rice. Make sure to cover the whole bowl!
  2. Now even spread the beans on top of your rice
  3. Put your grilled chicken on next, make sure to spread it evenly so you can layer the last ingredients on top of it
  4. Finally, garnish the bowl with an even layer of Salsa, lettuce, corn, and anything else you'd like to add to it
  5. Bon Apetit!
